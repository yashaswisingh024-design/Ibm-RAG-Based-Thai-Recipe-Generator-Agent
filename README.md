## 🌟 Overview

Starting a business requires extensive research, planning, financial analysis, and technical decision-making. Most aspiring entrepreneurs struggle with scattered information and lack of expert guidance.

**LaunchPilot AI** acts as an **AI Co-Founder**, autonomously researching, planning, analyzing, and generating everything needed to transform an idea into an investor-ready startup.

Unlike traditional chatbots, LaunchPilot uses a **multi-agent architecture** where specialized AI agents collaborate to perform complex business planning tasks.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Entrepreneurs often face challenges such as:

- Difficulty validating startup ideas
- Lack of market research
- Poor competitor analysis
- No structured business roadmap
- Limited access to funding information
- Time-consuming documentation

## 💡 Our Solution

LaunchPilot AI automates the complete startup planning lifecycle using **Agentic AI**.

Users simply describe their startup idea.

Example:

The AI autonomously performs:

✅ Market Research

✅ Competitor Analysis

✅ Financial Planning

✅ Technical Architecture

✅ Go-To-Market Strategy

✅ Startup Roadmap

✅ Investor Pitch Generation
